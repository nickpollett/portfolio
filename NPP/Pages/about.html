About

The man behind the camera

Nick Pollett got his start in photography in 2015. His spirit of adventure, and the drive to create something memorable and beautiful pushed him to develop his skills. Travel and Landscape photography are where he thrives.

Nick is always looking for a good excuse to get out there, meet new people, and gain meaningful experiences. His passion for photography drove him to build his own small business and is now providing quality images and memories for people all over the Prairies.
